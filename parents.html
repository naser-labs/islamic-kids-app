Parent Guide

Teach together with kindness

Simple prompts, respectful language, and short sessions to keep kids curious and comfortable.

How to use the app

1) Pick a short lesson

Lessons take about 5 minutes. Start with Foundations and follow the numbers, or let your child choose a topic.

2) Read it together

Keep it warm and positive. Pause for questions and relate ideas to your family's daily life.

3) Try the quick quiz

Use the quiz as a game. Celebrate effort over perfection, and revisit any tricky parts gently.

Conversation prompts

Connect to feelings

Ask: "How does this make you feel?" or "When did you see this in real life?"

Keep it gentle

Use calm tones. If a topic is sensitive, remind them Allah is Most Merciful.

Celebrate practice

Notice small actions: "I liked how you remembered to say Bismillah."

Model curiosity

It's okay to say "I don't know." Look up answers together when needed.

Safety and respect

Age-friendly

Language stays simple and supportive, focusing on kindness and basic beliefs.

Short and calm

Lessons are quick to reduce overwhelm and keep attention joyful.

Your pace

Pause, repeat, or skip questions anytime. Kids learn best when they feel safe.
