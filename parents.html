Parent & Guardian Guide

Supporting independent learning

A conversation starter, not a grading tool. Encourage discussion and critical thinking.

How to use this site

1) Let them choose

Teens can browse lessons independently. Recommend starting with Foundations or letting them explore topics that interest them.

2) Review together (optional)

If they want to discuss what they learned, review the lesson together. Ask open-ended questions.

3) Use quizzes as a check-in

Quizzes are self-check tools, not tests. Results can spark conversations about what they understood and what needs more thought.

Conversation starters

Ask open-ended questions

Privacy & trust

No accounts required

No sign-up, no login, no password. Just open and learn.

No tracking or ads

Progress is saved locally on the device. Nothing is sent to servers or third parties.

Self-paced learning

Teens can pause, repeat, or skip lessons. They control their learning journey.
